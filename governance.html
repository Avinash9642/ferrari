BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Pursuant to Article 14 of the Company’s Articles of Association, the Board of Directors –composed
of 11 directors, including both executive and non-executive – is responsible for the management
and strategic direction of the Group.

JOHN ELKANN

Executive Chairman

John Elkann is Chief Executive Officer of EXOR and Chairman of Stellantis N.V..

As the chairman and CEO of Exor N.V., he has successfully steered the company to new heights, overseeing a diverse portfolio that includes global brands like Ferrari, Stellantis, and CNH Industrial. Born into the renowned Agnelli family, Elkann embraced the legacy of his ancestors with determination and vision. His astute decision-making and forward-thinking mindset have played pivotal roles in the transformation and growth of Exor's businesses across various sectors, ranging from automotive to luxury goods.

BENEDETTO VIGNA

Chief Executive Officer

Benedetto Vigna is Chief Executive Officer since September 2021.

Benedetto Vigna is the current CEO of Ferrari. He was born in Potenza, Italy in 1969 and graduated as a physicist from the University of Pisa in 1993. He then began working for STMicroelectronics, where he held a number of senior positions in the semiconductor industry. In 2021, he was appointed CEO of Ferrari, becoming the first non-family member to lead the company in its 70-year history. He is a member of the World Economic Forum's Global Future Council on Advanced Manufacturing and has been named one of the "Top 100 Global Thinkers" by Foreign Policy magazine.

PIERO FERRARI

Vice Chairman and Non-Executive Director

Mr. Piero Ferrari has been Vice Chairman of Ferrari S.p.A. since 1988.

Piero Ferrari is an Italian billionaire businessman and sport personality. He is the second and only living son of Enzo Ferrari, and a 10.23% owner of the Ferrari automotive company, of which he is the vice chairman. He owns 13.2% of the Ferretti Group. Piero Ferrari began working at Ferrari in the early 1970s. He held a variety of positions, including manager of the Formula 1 team and head of the marketing department. In 1988, he was appointed vice chairman of Ferrari. He is the chairman of the Centro Dino Ferrari, a research center for muscular and neurodegenerative diseases. He is also a member of the board of directors of the Ferretti Group, a luxury yacht manufacturer.

DELPHINE ARNAULT

Non-Executive Director

Arnault has been involved in Ferrari since 2015, when she was appointed to the board of directors.

Delphine Arnault was born in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, in 1975. She studied at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne in Switzerland and at the London School of Economics. After graduating, she worked as a consultant for McKinsey & Company. Arnault joined LVMH in 2000. In 2013, she was appointed executive vice president of Louis Vuitton. In 2023, Arnault was appointed chairwoman and chief executive officer of Dior. She is the first woman to hold this position at the company. Arnault is also a member of the board of directors of LVMH.

FRANCESCA BELLETTINI

Non-Executive Director

Since September 2013, Francesca Bellettini is President and Chief Executive Officer of Yves Saint Laurent (part of the Kering Group), based in France.

Francesca Bellettini is a respected figure in the fashion industry, renowned for her contributions as the CEO of Saint Laurent, a renowned luxury fashion brand. With her exceptional leadership skills and business acumen, Bellettini has played a pivotal role in the brand's continued success and growth. Under Bellettini's guidance, Saint Laurent has flourished, achieving significant milestones and solidifying its position as a global fashion powerhouse. Beyond her achievements within the fashion industry, Francesca Bellettini is recognized for her philanthropic endeavors.

EDDY CUE

Non-Executive Director

Eddy Cue is Apple’s senior vice president of Services, reporting to CEO Tim Cook.

Eddy Cue is a prominent executive in the technology industry, best known for his contributions to Apple Inc. With a distinguished career spanning over three decades at the company, Cue has played a vital role in shaping and driving Apple's success. As Senior Vice President of Internet Software and Services, Cue has been instrumental in developing and expanding Apple's online services ecosystem. He has overseen the development of transformative products and services such as Apple Music, iCloud, Apple Pay, and the App Store, which have revolutionized the way we consume content, communicate, and interact with technology.

SERGIO DUCA

Senior Non-Executive Director

Sergio Duca is a former Ferrari executive who served as the company's chief marketing officer from 2015 to 2021.

Mr. Duca graduated with honors in Economics and Business from University Bocconi in Milan. Mr. Duca is member of the Statutory Auditors of Ferrovie dello Stato Italiane S.p.A. since 2022, independent director of OSAI Automation System S.p.A. since November 2020 and director of Tofaş Türk Otomobil Fabrikası Anonim Şirketi, as well as Chairperson of the corporate governance committee, member of the risk management committee and member of the audit committee of the board of directors of Tofaş Türk Otomobil Fabrikası Anonim Şirketi.

JOHN GALANTIC

Non-Executive Director

John Galantic is President and Chief Operating Officer of Chanel Inc.

Galantic obtained a Bachelor’s degree from Tufts University and Master’s degree in Business Administration from Harvard Business School. He began his career at Procter and Gamble and worked in various Marketing and Sales roles in Italy, the UK and US. After stints at Glaxo SmithKline in global Marketing and at Coty Beauty, as President of Coty Americas, he joined Chanel in 2006. He joined the board of Chanel in 2018. Galantic has also been on the board of Bacardi Limited since 2011. Since 2017, he has been on the board of the Chanel Fondation, a philanthropic organization focused on women and girls.

MARIA PATRIZIA GRIECO

Non-Executive Director

JMaria Patrizia Grieco has been the Chairperson of the Board of Directors of Anima Holding since March 2023.

Having graduating in Law from the University of Milan, she started her career in 1977 at Italtel, where in 1994 she became chief of the Legal and General Affairs directorate. In 1999, she was appointed General Manager with the task of reorganizing and repositioning the company, and in 2002 she became Chief Executive Officer. From 2008 to 2013 she was Chief Executive Officer of Olivetti, where she also held the role of Chairperson from 2011. She has been a member of the Board of Directors of Fiat Industrial, CIR and Endesa S.A. and currently serves on the Board of Ferrari and Amplifon.

ADAM KESWICK

Non-Executive Director

Mr Keswick first joined the Jardine Matheson Group in 2001 and was appointed to the Board of Jardine Matheson in 2007.

He was Deputy Managing Director of Jardine Matheson from 2012 to 2016, and became chairman of Matheson & Co. in 2016. Mr Keswick is a director of Dairy Farm, Hongkong Land, Jardine Strategic and Mandarin Oriental. He is also a Director of Ferrari NV, and Vice-Chairman of the Supervisory Board of Rothschild &Co., and is a Director of Yabuli China Entrepreneurs Forum. Adam is also a Non-Executive Member of the Board of Directors and Member of the Remuneration Committee of Schindler Holdings Ltd (Switzerland).

MIKE VOLPI

Non-Executive Director

Mike Volpi is a General Partner at Index Ventures.

Mike has a B.S in Mechanical Engineering and an M.S. in Manufacturing Systems Engineering from Stanford, and an M.B.A. from the Stanford Graduate School of Business. He currently serves on the Global Advisory Board of Stanford's Knight Hennessy Scholars program. Mike joined Index in 2009 to establish the firm's North American activities. Mike invests primarily in enterprise software and artificial intelligence. He is currently serving on the boards of Aurora, Confluent, Clickhouse, Scale, Sonos, and Wealthfront, among others. Mike was previously a director of Ericsson and Fiat Chrysler Automotive.
